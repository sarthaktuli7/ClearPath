COURSE EXPLANATION — LECTURE 11
R-squared measures the proportion of variance explained by the model, but it increases automatically when you add more predictors — even if they add no real value. Adjusted R-squared corrects for this by penalizing unnecessary variables. Use adjusted R-squared when comparing models with different numbers of predictors.
